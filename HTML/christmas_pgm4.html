Christmas Festival

Christmas is both a divine religious holiday and a worldwide cultural and commercial phenomenon Christians celebrate Christmas day as the anniversary of the birth of Jesus of Nazareth, a spiritual leader whose teachings form the basis of their Myth. Christmas is one of the major festivals celebrated by the people across the globe.

“Christmas is Joy ,holy Joy, an inner joy of light and peace”.

It is also known as the Feast day of Christ and celebrated in the honour of birth of Jesus Christ.
